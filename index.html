Giselle

We are Giselle, and our project, Giselle, is designed to provide mid-sized retailer clothing stores with a robust online platform, eliminating the hassle of creating and maintaining a website. Giselle integrates various cutting-edge technologies to enhance the shopping experience for users by offering a single platform to browse multiple clothing stores, all in one place.

Our solution leverages semantic search and image search to bring an immersive shopping experience to users, making it a win-win for both retailers and shoppers.

How We Used Ballerina:

We utilized Ballerina as the backbone of our platform, handling:

  • Database interactions
  • gRPC services for communication with Python servers running ML tasks
  • GraphQL servers for managing large schemas
  • REST APIs for sending and retrieving data
  • Authentication services via Auth0
